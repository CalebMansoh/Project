DOBIDI

Dobidi Smart FarmaTech is an AgricTech project that is solving the challenge of monitoring with the agric sector. We provide a platform for Agribusinesses to monitor farms and solve post harvest loses. We connect farmers to consumer market by advertising their produce to readily available consumers.

With DOBIDI FarmaTech, default on the part of farmers are not worry again

Monitoring and Supervison

monitoring software (SaaS) as a service for all Agribusinesses with our customized platform to monitor all farm activities and farmers and providing well trained agric extension officers and supervisors to guide, train and monitor farmers through real time farm alerts and reports
