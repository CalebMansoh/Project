DOBIDI

Imagine realizing that the tubers and vegetables you once savored now taste different. This change points to a deeper issue: the infringementof our food sovereignty due to underutilized technologies. Developed by the Awaso Stemnovation Club, DOBIDI is an advanced, automated precision farm equipped with state-of-the-art climate monitoring, self-irrigating systems, and real time soil analysis. Powered by solar energy and stand -alone batteries, it uses GPS and AI to maximize water efficiency, predict weather patterns, and connect farmers directly to consumers via an online marketplace. Designed to be user-friendly and scalable, DOBIDI aims to combat hunger, promote sustainable agriculture, and revitalize lands impacted by illegal mining for agriculture. It's not just a smart farm; it's a step towards a greener, more self sufficient Ghana.
